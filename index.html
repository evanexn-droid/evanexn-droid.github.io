About Evan Wormhole

One year ago , Evan Wormhole bought Logic Pro and created their first demo 'really don't love' in 12 hours . In the 12 months since that moment , Evan has been working with great passion to experiment with and harness the possibilities of music production to create boundless vessels to bear songs of anxious futures , tumultuous memories , and their quest for a fun , funky , fulfilling lifestyle in the present . When they're not singing of their own afflictions , Evan often embodies the thoughts of a generation who's voices are lost on those in charge , and holds hope and cautious optimism close to their heart .
