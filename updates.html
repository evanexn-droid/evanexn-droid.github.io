11/06/2026 - The Rebirth of EvanWormhole.com and the Release of Invisible Thumbs (Feat. No Further Services) !

This June , Evan Wormhole is cementing their presence on the world wide web. Last week, I kicked off the month with an update to the mixing and mastering of Dashcam Footage , bringing it up to scratch with the other songs to be featured on my upcoming mixtape. This week, I am excited to share with you all this exciting new chapter for EvanWormhole.com, keeping some signature elements of the site i created in April last year while making it more purpose built for those of you interested in my music. But this is not the only exciting news this Friday, as I am thrilled to announce that my latest and greatest single yet, Invisible Thumbs , will be released next Friday , continuing this June takeover of mine. The song features a dear friend of mine Sam 'The Band' Cundy (a.k.a the purple one) of hit Worthing Indie Darlings No Further Services , who brings a vocal performace that will cradle your soul in its hands while shaking with a fear of the Invisible Thumbs. After seeing him in person to record this song, I am pleased to confirm that Cundy indeed does not have invisible hands himself. Invisible Thumbs has been in development since August of last year , and I am so excited to finally share the finished version with you all.
